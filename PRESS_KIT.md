# Mindful Planner - Press Kit

## About Mindful Daily Planner

**Tagline:** Plan Your Day with Purpose, Not Pressure

**One-liner:** A daily planner that balances productivity with wellbeing using 7 unique planning zones.

### Product Description

Mindful Daily Planner helps you organize your day without burnout. Unlike traditional planners that focus only on tasks, we help you balance:

- **Deep Work:** Focus time for important projects
- **Quick Wins:** Small tasks that build momentum
- **Make It Happen:** Events, meetings, and deadlines
- **Recharge Zones:** Breaks, exercise, meditation
- **Little Joys:** Hobbies and passion projects
- **Reflections:** Daily learning and gratitude
- **Progress Log:** Track what you accomplished

**Current Status:** Free during beta (launching Q1 2026)

### Key Features
- 7-zone planning system for balanced productivity
- Timeline view showing your entire day
- Core memories feature for reflection
- Mobile-responsive web app
- Privacy-focused (no ads, no data selling)

**Q: What makes Mindful Planner different?**
A: We're the only planner that explicitly balances work with wellbeing, treating rest and joy as equally important to tasks.

**Q: Who is this for?**
A: Anyone who feels overwhelmed by traditional productivity tools. Especially helpful for knowledge workers, creatives, students, and people with ADHD.

**Q: Is it free?**
A: Yes during beta. Future pricing will include a free tier with premium features for $5-10/month.

A: Currently web-based (works on all devices). Native mobile apps coming 2026.
